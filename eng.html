About the tour!

We invite you on exciting tours from Almaty to amazing and beautiful places in Kazakhstan:

Black Canyon, Kolsai and Kaindy.

Black Canyon

- a picturesque place with breathtaking views and unusual rock formations. Here you can enjoy the grandeur of nature and take unique photographs.

Kolsai Lakes

is a chain of three lakes surrounded by dense forests and green meadows. The water in the lakes is crystal clear, and the surrounding landscape is striking in its beauty and tranquility. An ideal place for hiking and picnics.

Lake Kaindy

- famous for its underwater forest, where submerged trees create a fantastic landscape. The water in the lake is incredibly clear, allowing you to see the amazing underwater world.

Join our tours and discover these beautiful corners of Kazakhstan! We guarantee an unforgettable experience and comfortable conditions for every traveler. Don't miss the opportunity to see the unique natural wonders of your homeland!

Tour plan:

01

Convenient collection

We will pick you up from your hotel. We have a comfortable minibus that can accommodate up to 10 people. Our English speaking guide is always with you.

02

Adventure in the Black Canyon

We reach the Black Canyon, where the Charyn River breaks through the rocks and cliffs. Just wow! You have 10-15 minutes to take a photo.

03

Let's explore Kolsai Lakes

The lakes are called "Pearl" Northern Tien Shan". Lakes surrounded by coniferous forest from Tien Shan spruce and have a depth of up to 50 m. Among the fauna representatives there are Rainbow trout. You have 3 hours free time.

04

Lunch break

You have 40-45 minutes for lunch.

05

Stop at Lake Kaindy.

The lake is interesting because after the valley was flooded, the forest growing in it was not destroyed, but formed a unique landscape. Regular cars do not drive up to Kainda itself, so we change to a larger car and drive for 3 hours.

06

Return to Almaty

We're going to our beautiful city Almaty.
